Love Is On The Bridge

A love lock is a padlock that couples lock to a bridge, fence, gate or any other public fixture to symbolize love and commitment.

Arguably, the most famous love lock bridge is the Pont des Arts in Paris, France. In June 2014, part of the bridge collapsed under the weight of all the locks. This lead authorities to cut and remove all the locks from the bridge.

Don't worry, here are 7 other bridges across Europe where couples can still "lock" their love... once it's safe to travel!

  • Most Ljubavi | Vrnjačka Banja, Serbia
  • Hohenzollern Bridge | Cologne, Germany
  • Shoreditch High Street Station | London, England
  • John Lennon Wall | Prague, Czech Republic
  • Butchers’ Bridge | Ljubljana, Slovenia
  • Makartsteg Footbridge | Salzburg, Austria
  • Elizabeth’s Bridge | Cluj Napoca, Romania
